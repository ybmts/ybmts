Overview.

Since its inception, Midtown-Star Oilfield Services has specialized in the petroleum industry. We supply equipment, materials and services to a wide array of projects encompassing oil and gas exploration, drilling, production, refineries, oil and gas pipelines and LPG terminals.

With more than three decades of experience, a growing global network of partners providing state-of-the-art technology and products, and deep industrial expertise, we deliver unparalleled value for our clients.
